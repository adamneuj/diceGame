Rules of the diceGame

How to win the game:
Hit 1000 points.

How to lose the game:
Go over 1000 points or run out of turns.

How to play:

Start by picking a dice.
Each dice will give you potential points corresponding to their multiplier.
For example: A 20-sided dice has a 32x multiplier giving you the potential of being able to get 640 points in one turn.
After picking the dice, you will not earn any points yet.

Then you will play a game of rock, paper, scissors.
If won, you will earn points to your score.
If lost, you will earn no points and go back to picking a dice.
If tied, you will play rock, paper, scissors again.

If you go over 1000 points, you will have 2 turns to bring the score back down to 1000.
The game will be played the same except that the dice will take away from the score.
